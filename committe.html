Committee

Meet Our Esteemed Committee Members

Prof. Sashank Prasad

Associate Professor, Harvard Medical School

Associate Professor, Harvard Medical School, Philadelphia, USA. Director, Partners Neurology Residency Program. Recognized expert in Neuro-Ophthalmology and Medical Education.

Dr. Ashish Atre

Senior Consultant Neuro-Radiology and Paediatric Imaging

Dr. Ashish Atre is a Senior Consultant in Neuro-Radiology and Paediatric Imaging at Pune. He is Chief Radiologist at Star Imaging and Research Centre and Hon. Associate Professor at Sassoon General Hospital and BJ Medical College, Pune.

Dr. Sharath Kumar

Interventional Neuro-Radiologist

Dr. Sharath Kumar is a renowned Interventional Neuro-Radiologist who has performed over 5000 Endovascular Neurosurgery procedures. He has extensive clinical knowledge and skills with vast experience in the interdisciplinary branch of Radiology.

Dr. Catherine Albin

Neurointensivist, Emory University School of Medicine

Dr. Catherine Albin, MD is a Neurointensivist from Emory University School of Medicine where she is a member of the Department of Neurocritical Care. Her research focuses on educational innovations in Acute Neurologic Emergencies.

Prof. Neeraj Kumar

Professor of Neurology, Mayo Clinic Alix School of Medicine

Dr. Neeraj Kumar, MD, FAAN, is a Professor of Neurology at the Mayo Clinic Alix School of Medicine in Rochester, Minnesota. His clinical interests include disorders of the spine, neurotoxicology, and neurological conditions related to nutrition.

Prof. Steven L Lewis

Secretary-General, World Federation of Neurology (WFN)

Dr. Lewis is the Secretary-General of the World Federation of Neurology (WFN), Chair of the WFN Education Committee, and the Editor of World Neurology. He has also been the recipient of various prestigious awards for his contributions to neurologic education.

Neurological Society of Pune (NSP) is a registered society of neurologists and neurosurgeons, established in 2010 to propagate advances in neurology and set high standards in neurology education.
