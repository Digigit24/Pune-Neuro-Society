Formation (2010)

NSP was established in year 2010 for propagating advances in field of neurology.

It consisted of Neurophysician and Neurosurgeons practicing in city of Pune. There used to have once monthly neuromeets where different hospitals will present their cases. These clinical meets are continuing till date. These meets have very interactive discussions. The first big conference organised under banner of NSP was annual national conference of Indian Academy of Neurology, IANCON 2011 in Pune. After successfully organising this conference, it was decided to have bigger format of neuromeets and thus started the Approach meet.

The Approach meet is organised once in 2 years and are attended by neurologists from all corners of India. It is on of its kind meeting in neurology which has been on national stage.

Activities

Our Activities

NSP regularly organizes Invited lectures from eminent speakers in neurology, updating practicing neurologists in the city with the latest advancements.

Approach Meeting for Postgraduates

The Approach meeting for postgraduate students in neurology is another benchmark in neurology education, organized annually to foster learning among future neurologists.

Governance & Organization

NSP is a registered society under the Societies Act, with its daily operations managed by an executive committee elected through a fair election process among society members. The society currently has over 70 members.

Commitment to Excellence

NSP has set exceptionally high standards in neurology education over the years, continuously evolving to provide quality education and expertise.

Public Awareness Drive

Recently, NSP has initiated a public awareness campaign about various neurological diseases, utilizing social media platforms to stay aligned with technological advancements.
